**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [AWS Foundations, Networking, Security, and Hands-on Labs](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Hands-on with AWS Compute (EC2, Auto Scaling, Load Balancing), Monitoring (CloudWatch), Backup, and Storage Services](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [AWS Security & Database Services (IAM, Cognito, KMS, RDS, Aurora, Redshift, ElastiCache)](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [AWS Data Analytics, NoSQL, and AI Lifecycle Exploration Week](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Project Initialization, .NET Integration & Hybrid Network Architecture (VPC/DNS)](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Deploy Decoupled Architecture, ECS Orchestration & Secure API](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Strengthen Infrastructure Architecture: Network, Storage, Database & Compute](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Doing task H...](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Building .NET Aspire Platform, Integrating AWS Cognito & Wallet Management Logic](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Developing Advanced Transaction Logic & Integrating RabbitMQ/MassTransit](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Reporting System, Alerts & Automated Processing](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [System Optimization, Enhanced Security & Deployment Readiness](1.12-week12/)
